## OVERVIEW

This guide explains the complete workflow from initial creator outreach through deal closing and contract execution.

**Full Flow:**
1. **Outreach** - Contact creators
2. **Lead** - Creator shows interest
3. **Deal** - Negotiate terms
4. **Contract** - Finalize agreement
5. **Campaign** - Execute work
6. **Payment** - Complete transaction

**Timeline:** Typically 2-4 weeks from outreach to signed contract

```
{{sender_name}}
```

## PHASE 2: LEAD QUALIFICATION

### When Creator Replies

**Notification:**
- Email notification (if enabled)
- In-app notification
- Awaiting reply list updated

**Review Reply:**
1. Navigate to Inbox ‚Üí Awaiting Reply
2. Open creator's response
3. Read interest level:
   - **Hot:** "Yes, interested! Let's discuss"
   - **Warm:** "Tell me more about the campaign"
   - **Cold:** "Not interested right now"

---

### Qualifying the Lead

**Hot Lead (Create Deal Immediately):**
- Creator explicitly interested
- Asks about compensation/deliverables
- Ready to discuss terms

**Warm Lead (Nurture First):**
- Creator wants more info
- Not ready to commit
- Send campaign details, follow up in 2-3 days

**Cold Lead (Mark as Closed):**
- Creator declines
- Not a fit for campaign
- Archive outreach

---

### Creating Deal from Outreach

**Method 1: From Inbox**
1. Open creator's reply email
2. Click "Create Deal" button
3. Deal form pre-fills with context

**Method 2: From Outreach Dashboard**
1. Find creator in outreach campaign
2. Click "Convert to Deal"
3. Deal created, linked to outreach

**Deal Details Auto-Filled:**
- Contact: Creator name
- Source: Outreach campaign name
- Notes: Email conversation history
- Stage: "Lead" (initial stage)

## PHASE 3: DEAL MANAGEMENT

### Deal Pipeline Stages

**1. Lead** (Initial Interest)
- Creator responded positively
- Qualification in progress
- Next: Send detailed proposal

**2. Qualified** (Ready to Negotiate)
- Creator fits campaign criteria
- Budget range acceptable
- Next: Negotiate deliverables

**3. Proposal** (Terms Sent)
- Formal proposal sent
- Awaiting creator's response
- Next: Address questions, finalize terms

**4. Negotiation** (Back-and-Forth)
- Discussing compensation
- Adjusting deliverables
- Resolving objections
- Next: Reach agreement

**5. Won** (Deal Closed)
- Terms agreed upon
- Ready for contract
- Next: Generate contract

**6. Lost** (Deal Failed)
- Creator declined
- Budget mismatch
- Timing doesn't work

---

---

### Moving Deal Forward

**From Lead ‚Üí Qualified:**
1. Verify creator fits campaign
2. Confirm budget range
3. Update stage to "Qualified"
4. Task: Prepare formal proposal

**From Qualified ‚Üí Proposal:**
1. Draft proposal document:
   - Campaign overview
   - Deliverables (posts, stories, videos)
   - Compensation
   - Timeline
   - Content guidelines
2. Send proposal via email or platform
3. Update stage to "Proposal"
4. Task: Follow up in 2-3 days

**From Proposal ‚Üí Negotiation:**
1. Creator responds with questions/concerns
2. Log objections as notes
3. Update stage to "Negotiation"
4. Address each concern
5. Revise proposal if needed

**From Negotiation ‚Üí Won:**
1. Creator agrees to terms
2. Verbal/written confirmation received
3. Update stage to "Won"
4. Next: Generate contract

## PHASE 4: CONTRACT GENERATION

### When to Generate Contract

**Trigger:** Deal stage = "Won"

**Requirements:**
- All terms finalized
- Deliverables specified
- Compensation agreed
- Timeline confirmed

---

### Creating Contract

**Navigate to:** CRM ‚Üí Deals ‚Üí [Deal] ‚Üí "Create Contract"

**Contract Form:**

**1. Basic Info:**
- Contract title: "Nike x @fashionista - Summer Campaign"
- Brand: Nike
- Creator: @fashionista (auto-filled from deal)
- Deal value: $2,500 (auto-filled)

**2. Deliverables:**
- Add each deliverable:
  - "3 Instagram Feed Posts"
  - "2 Instagram Stories"
  - "1 TikTok Video"
- Specify due dates for each
- Add content guidelines/requirements

**3. Payment Terms:**
- Payment amount: $2,500
- Payment method: Bank transfer
- Payment schedule:
  - 50% upfront ($1,250)
  - 50% upon completion ($1,250)
- Payment deadline: 30 days after completion

**4. Campaign Timeline:**
- Start date: February 1, 2026
- End date: February 28, 2026
- Content due: February 15, 2026
- Final approval: February 20, 2026

**5. Additional Terms:**
- Usage rights (1 year, organic only)
- Exclusivity clause (no competing brands for 90 days)
- Approval process (2 rounds of revisions)
- Content ownership (brand owns final approved content)

**6. Legal Clauses (Optional):**
- Termination clause
- Confidentiality agreement
- Dispute resolution
- Governing law

---

### Contract Review

**Auto-Generated Preview:**
- PDF preview shows formatted contract
- All details formatted professionally
- Review for accuracy

**Edit if Needed:**
- Click "Edit" to modify terms
- Update deliverables
- Adjust payment schedule
- Regenerate preview

**Internal Approval (Optional):**
- Send to legal team for review
- Get manager approval if required
- Make final edits
- Ready to send

### Sending Contract

1. Click "Send Contract"

4. Send

**Method 2: Platform Signing (if enabled)**
1. Click "Send for Signature"
2. Creator receives notification
3. Creator reviews in platform
4. Creator signs electronically
5. Both parties receive signed copy

**Status:** Contract status = "Sent"

### Contract Tracking

**Contract Statuses:**
- **Draft** - Being created

**Notifications:**
- Email when creator views contract
- Email when creator signs contract
- Reminder if no action after 3 days

**Payment Milestones:**

- **Status:** Mark as "Paid" in system
- **Record:** Upload payment receipt

- **Trigger:** All deliverables approved
- **Action:** Process payment within 30 days
- **Status:** Mark as "Paid" in system

### Campaign Completion

**Post-Campaign:**
- Review campaign performance
- Compare deliverables vs contract
- Calculate ROI

## COMMON WORKFLOWS

### Fast-Track (Hot Lead)

**Timeline: 3-5 days**

1. **Day 1:** Outreach sent
2. **Day 1:** Creator replies "interested!"
3. **Day 1:** Create deal, send proposal
4. **Day 2:** Creator accepts proposal
5. **Day 2:** Move deal to "Won"
6. **Day 3:** Generate and send contract
7. **Day 5:** Creator signs contract
8. **Day 5:** Process upfront payment

**Keys to Speed:**
- Pre-approved budget
- Standard contract template
- Quick legal review
- Automated payment processing

---

### Standard Flow (Warm Lead)

**Timeline: 2-3 weeks**

1. **Week 1, Day 1:** Outreach sent
2. **Week 1, Day 3:** Creator replies with questions
3. **Week 1, Day 4:** Answer questions, create deal
4. **Week 1, Day 7:** Send formal proposal
5. **Week 2, Day 2:** Creator has concerns, negotiate
6. **Week 2, Day 5:** Agreement reached, move to "Won"
7. **Week 2, Day 6:** Generate contract
8. **Week 3, Day 1:** Creator signs contract
9. **Week 3, Day 2:** Process upfront payment

---

### Complex Deal (Multiple Rounds)

**Timeline: 4-6 weeks**

1. **Week 1:** Initial outreach, creator interested
2. **Week 2:** Detailed proposal sent, creator has questions
3. **Week 3:** Multiple negotiation rounds (budget, deliverables)
4. **Week 4:** Agreement reached after adjustments
5. **Week 5:** Contract drafted, legal review, revisions
6. **Week 6:** Contract signed, payment processed

**Common Complexities:**
- Budget negotiations
- Multiple deliverable options
- Exclusivity terms
- Usage rights negotiations
- Legal review delays

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Creator Not Responding to Outreach

**After 3 days:**
- Send friendly follow-up
- Reference original message
- Add value (new campaign details)

**After 7 days:**
- Final follow-up
- "Still interested? Let me know"

**After 14 days:**
- Mark as closed
- Move to next creator

---

### Deal Stalled in Negotiation

**Common Causes:**
- Budget mismatch
- Deliverable concerns
- Timeline conflicts
- Competing offers

**Solutions:**
1. Schedule call to discuss
2. Ask "What would make this work for you?"
3. Find creative solutions (adjust deliverables)
4. Know your walk-away point

---

### Creator Won't Sign Contract

**Common Reasons:**
- Legal concerns
- Unclear terms
- Better offer elsewhere
- Changed mind

**Actions:**
1. Ask what's holding them back
2. Address specific concerns
3. Clarify confusing terms
4. Offer to discuss with their agent
5. If unresponsive after 7 days, mark as "Lost"

---

### Payment Delays

**Causes:**
- Finance team backlog
- Missing invoice
- Payment approval needed
- Bank transfer issues

**Prevention:**
- Set payment reminders
- Track payment milestones
- Upload receipts immediately
- Communicate delays proactively
